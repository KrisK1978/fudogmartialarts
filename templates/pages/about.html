Welcome to Fu Dog Martial Arts!

Who are we and what we do?

Fu Dog Martial Arts is a new combat gym, aimed at providing the local community with a safe, welcoming and respectful environment to work out and achieve their fitness goals.

Regardless of age, gender, experience and skill, Fu Dog Martial Arts provides the facilities and coaching experience to support personal growth through building confidence and self-belief.

Head coach...

Iain Feenan, has over 20 years experience in judo and combat sport.Iain trained full time and competed as an international judo athlete, since retiring from competitive sport, he transitioned to MMA and now trains as a pro mma fighter.
